*This bibliography provides comprehensive coverage of Hanako-san research, spanning folklore, psychology, education, media studies, and cultural analysis. The legend's accessibility and cultural significance have generated substantial academic and popular interest, making it one of the most documented Japanese urban legends.*

---

**Research Note**: Hanako-san's widespread recognition and long history have produced both popular and academic sources. For serious research, combining ethnographic field work with children, analysis of media representations, and understanding of Japanese school culture provides the most complete picture. The legend continues to evolve, requiring ongoing documentation of contemporary variations and new media adaptations.
